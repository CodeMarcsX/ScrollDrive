The BMW M1 is an iconic BMW sports car, launched in the late 1970s, with a mid-mounted engine and a focus on racing. Produced in very few units, it has become one of the rarest and most important models in the brand's history.

The BMW M2 is a compact sports car from the M division, with a powerful engine, rear-wheel drive and an aggressive look. It balances extreme performance and everyday use, while maintaining the brand's racing DNA.

The BMW M3 is a legendary sports sedan from the M division, combining a powerful engine, precise chassis, and aggressive styling. It's versatile, suitable for everyday driving, and delivers real track performance.

The BMW M4 is a sports coupe from the M division, with a total focus on performance, striking design, and sharp handling. It combines brute force, precise control, and driving excitement, while maintaining comfort.

The BMW M5 is a high-performance executive sedan that combines luxury and impressive power. With brutal acceleration, advanced technology and premium comfort, it delivers supercar performance without sacrificing sophistication.
